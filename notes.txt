					--- DATASET ---
HAM10000 has 10000 training images
	- 10,015 dermatoscopic images
	- metadata file (CSV)

DATASET LINK: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/kmader/skin-cancer-mnist-ham10000

More that 50% of the lesion are confirmed through histopathology.
There are 7 classes of skin cancer (not perfecting balanced):
	1. Melanocytic nevi (nv)
	2. Melanoma (mel)
	3. Benign keratosis-like lesion (bkl)
	4. Basa cell carcinoma (bbc)
	5. Actinic keratoses (akiec)
	6. Vascular lesions (vas)
	7. Dermatofibroma (df)
